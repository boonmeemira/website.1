Cactus species and their characteristics

Astrophytum

Round shape with white dots resembling stars

Euphorbia

Its trunk is triangular or square in shape

Golden Barrel

It is round or epic in size

Opuntia

The leaves are flat and have small, velvet-like hairs

Haworthia

Bright green or dark green leaves with white or clear stripes at small spots or veins on the leaves.

Blue Myrtle-Cactus

It is a cactus that stands out with its stem color and tall, straight shape.
